    Meteorology, Specifically Tropical Storm Tracking

    In 2016-2017 I became increasingly interested in tracking tropical activity in the Atlantic. Being a resident of a Florida, a hurricane prone region, I thought it would be good to be in the know about the tropics. I started reading a weather blog known as Category 6, and I frequented the comments of the blog posts where other readers would discuss tropical disturbances, meteorology, and atmospheric science. Eventually, I learned a lot about how tropical systems form and develop, and I picked up terminology used in the field. During the hurricane season, I check forecast models, satellite imagery, and the blog almost every day.
